Chess Squares

Ingredients:

  • 1 Box yellow cake mix
  • 3 eggs
  • 8oz of cream cheese, softened
  • 1 stick of butter
  • 1lb of powederd sugar

Directions

1

Preheat oven to 300°. Spray a 9"x13" dish with cooking spray.

2

Mix cake mix, melted butter, and one egg to a soft dough. Press into the bottom of the pan.

3

Mix powdered sugar, softened cream cheese and remaining two eggs until smooth, about 1-2 minutes. Pour on top of crust.

4

Bake at 400° for 40-50 minutes until top is golden brown. Do not overcook.
