From Chess Pie
To Chess Squares

History

Chess pies are a Southern specialty that has a simple filling of eggs, sugar, butter, and a small amount of flour. Some recipes include cornmeal and others are made with vinegar. Flavorings, such as vanilla, lemon juice, or chocolate are also added to vary the basic recipe. Some where down the road people decided to change chess pie into gooey, flaky, sugary, buttery chess squares.

This recipe isn’t for you if you avoid sweet, creamy cookie bars with a delicate flaky upper crust and a soft browned cake-like bottom crust.
